Biography

September Again crafts dense, cinematic songs where alternative rock, metal and ambient sound design collide. On Seven Years, the band turns each track into a short film, blending heavy riffs, fragile vocals and visual storytelling into one continuous emotional thread.

The project pushes as far on the visual side as it does on the sound: every release comes with a dedicated film, carefully directed and edited by the band, exploring themes of identity, grief, control and release.
